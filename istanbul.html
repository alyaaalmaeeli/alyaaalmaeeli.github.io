(Turkey) is one of the most beautiful tourist countries in the world where tourists come from everywhere. It is the largest city in Turkey and the capital. I visited turkey in summer 2018. There are a lot of activities to do in Istanbul and a lot of places to visit as well. “Haia Sophia” Grand Mosque it is surrounded by the Sea of Marmara. I visited other famous mosque “Blue Mosque” or Sultan Ahmet camii. Its one of the biggest mosques in Istanbul with wonderful interior and exterior designs. “Taksim square” is a high rises streat contains shops , restaurants and coffee shops. Moreover for shopping, We visited the “Grand Bazar” it’s a traditional grand market and the biggest in the entire world. Also, We didn’t miss going to “Minia-Turk” It is one of the world's largest miniature parks. This park represent turkey in a mini size, Also, it contains 135 models of famous historical places in Turkey.

section

“Bosphorus Bridge” one of the most important landmark in Istanbul. It spans across the Bosphorus Strait between Ortakoy district. The bridge connects the Asian and European section of Istanbul. The bridge costs around $200 million to build. You can see the bridge whenever you are in Istanbul. The bridge located in “Marmara sea” the sea separates the country's European and Asian part. Its also known as the smallest sea. We got a boat ride in a perfect weather and view of Istanbul. Moreover, For entertainment we visited “Vialand” or “Istanbul theme park ” it is a huge complex containing a huge theme park which is one of the best ones in the world, shopping and entertainment areas. Located in the Europe district of Istanbul, it is a great place to spend a full day out with your family and kids. We really enjoyed this visit and had a great time. Lastly, The end of this beautiful trip was an adventure in the mountains ( Safari ) of Turkey. We’ve rented a safari car and had a ride on one of the highest mountains in turkey. It was the first time for me to do this adventure and I never regret it. Finally, That was a wrap of our trip to Istanbul, Turkey.
