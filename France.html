( Paris ) the city of lights known as one of the most beautiful cities in the world. As Thomas Jefferson said : “A walk about Paris will provide lessons in history, beauty, and in the point of life..” Paris is home for History and art lovers, where they can find both in one of the museums in paris. Tourism in Paris is like living in the old classic generation as if you were in beautiful old films. According to the world population review France got the first place for the most visited countries and tourism in 2019 ( 89.4 Million ) visitor per the year. There is a lot of places to visit and discover in Paris. “ Disney World “ the place where my dream came true. The place of dreams and imagination, Once you enter Disneyworld, you do enter into a magic kingdom. it was the first place we visited when we touch-down in France. We spent 3 days in this beautiful kingdom although it wasn’t enough. It was an indescribable feeling to meet the characters that every child loved, Disney princesses and the other characters, Watching the Palace that you have always seen in Disney movies.

It was a lovely experience with my cousins, We enjoyed playing, watching Disney shows and fireworks. Looking forward to visit the magical world soon again. After we completed our trip to Disney World, We took a train and headed to the second destination.. to the heart of Paris. “Eiffel Tower” the first thing that comes to your mind when you think about Paris. Eiffel Tower was opened on March 31, 1889 it took two years to build. The tower is 324 meters where you can enjoy the view of the city on top point of the tower. It’s located near to the Seine River. We took a boat cruise on the Seine and it was a relaxing moment enjoying the city of lights at night. Paris is known museums, there are 130 museum in the capital. I personally visited the most famous of them which it’s “ The Louver Museum ” the world’s largest museum with an extremely impressive art collection. it’s located in the Louver Palace. It’s the most wonderful museum I’ve visited. For the beauty of its exterior and interior design that combines classic and moderness of F It Contains Thousand pieces of arts and objects such as rare jewels, Diamonds and classic paintings.
