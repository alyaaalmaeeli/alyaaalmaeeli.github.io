( London ) the heart of the united kingdom and one of my favorite cities I have always dreamt to visit Since I was young. I visited London in August 2014 with my family, we explore lot of places in London. Hyde Park one of the royal and largest parks in London And it has been classified as one of the greatest city parks in the world. There is a lot of activities to do in Hyde Park, people enjoy picnicking which it’s a great way outdoor in the Hyde Park, Breathing fresh air and enjoying a great nature view with friends or family member. People also stay active in Hyde park by cycling, playing football , jogging and a lot of sport.

We enjoyed renting pedal boats on the Serpentine. We also visited the Big ben and London eye which are most famous places to visit in a trip to London. We enjoyed the view of the River cruise on the top point of London eye’s wheel. Moreover, We really had fun in the Madame Tussauds ( Wax museum ) it displays celebrities , Royal family members , Scientists and inventors. Furthermore , As I’m a Shopping lover I enjoyed the Oxford shopping Street which it is the world's biggest high street considers fashion brands , Restaurants , entertainment and the UK's iconic department stores including Selfridges and Marks & Spencer. We as visitors and tourist to London we don’t miss going to the most famous department store in London and one of Europe’s largest market department stores ( Harrods ), Where you find renowned luxury items of clothing, jewellery , houseware , packaged gifts , beauty items and many of them and many more. Finally , That was a brief of our lovely trip to London, looking forward to another visit to this beautiful city.
